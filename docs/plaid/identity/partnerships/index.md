# Identity Partners

#### Find providers who have partnered with Plaid to provide account ownership verification

=\*=\*=\*=

#### Overview

| Partner | Description |
| --- | --- |
| [Alloy](/docs/identity/partnerships/alloy/) | Offers identity data orchestration and credit decisioning to financial services companies looking to address fraud and uphold compliance (US only) |

The partners listed here include only those who provide an integration with Identity independently rather than as part of a money movement solution with Auth. For partners who use Plaid Identity as a complement to Plaid Auth, see [Auth Partnerships](/docs/auth/partnerships/).

For more information, see the list of partners in the [Plaid Dashboard](https://dashboard.plaid.com/developers/integrations).
